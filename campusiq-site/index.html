UniMate: The academic companion built for Ghanaian students

Track your CWA, manage your timetable, and build better study habits — all in one place. Built for KNUST, Legon, UCC, and universities across Ghana.

Everything you need to stay on top of your academics

UniMate integrates academic target-setting, timetable reminders, offline accessibility, and custom study streams into a clean, modern interface.

CWA / GPA Planner

Track your grades, set targets, and see your projected CWA in real time. Perfect for navigating Ghanaian grading systems.

Smart Timetable

Import your class schedule by photo and get reminders before every class. Never miss a lecture venue shift or slot.

Study Sessions

Time your study sessions with Normal and Pomodoro modes. Build a daily streak and foster deep concentration habits.

AI Study Plan

Get a personalised weekly study plan generated from your actual timetable and habits using advanced AI models.

Weekly Review

Receive an AI-generated narrative of your academic week — what went well and what topics to focus on next.

Works Offline

All your academic data stays securely on your device. No cloud sync, no sign-in barriers, and zero data cost.
